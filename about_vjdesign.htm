About VJdesigns


VJdesigns is a business that promotes artwork and someday may turn into a business. Founded in 2024, VJdesigns was founded by Victoria Yancey after having an assignment in college of building a website. Victoria Yancey graduated from a local private school in 2023 and is currently in the graphic design program at Brightpoint Community College. She hopes that VJdesigns will be a platform that will allow her to promote her artwork and to either get a job in the graphic design field or to one day start a graphic design business.
